About Blake

Kyle Reed is a Richmond, Virginia-based musician, songwriter, and self-described "old soul" whose performances blend classic rock, country, folk, soul, and modern acoustic favorites into an engaging, nostalgic experience. 

Originally from Florida, Kyle spent several seasons performing throughout the Fort Walton Beach and Destin area, appearing at popular venues including McGuire's Irish Pub, Brotula's Seafood House & Steamer, AJ's Seafood & Oyster Bar, The Other End, and The Green Door.

Drawing inspiration from artists such as Paul Simon, Bob Dylan, John Prine, Tom Petty, The Beatles, and The Head and the Heart, Kyle delivers a repertoire that balances timeless crowd favorites with thoughtful songwriter selections.
