About

Kyle Reed is a Richmond, Virginia-based musician, songwriter, and self-described "old soul".

Originally from Florida, Kyle spent several seasons performing throughout the Fort Walton Beach and Destin area before relocating to Richmond.

Drawing inspiration from Tom Petty, Bob Dylan, John Prine, Paul Simon, Sam Cooke, The Beatles, and The Head and the Heart, Kyle's performances blend familiar favorites with thoughtful songwriter selections.
